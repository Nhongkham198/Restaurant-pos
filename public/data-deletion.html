User Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated:

According to the Facebook Platform rules, we have to provide User Data Deletion Callback URL or Data Deletion Instructions URL. If you want to delete your activities for the POS System App, you can remove your information by following these steps:

How to Request Data Deletion

  1. Go to your Facebook Account's "Settings & Privacy". Click "Settings".
  2. Look for "Apps and Websites" and you will see all of the apps and websites you linked with your Facebook.
  3. Search and Click "POS System" in the search bar.
  4. Scroll and click "Remove".
  5. Congratulations, you have successfully removed your app activities.

Contact for Manual Deletion

If you wish to ensure all your data is permanently deleted from our servers beyond just the Facebook connection, please contact our support team:

  • Email: support@example.com
  • Subject: Data Deletion Request

We will process your request and delete your data within 30 days.
