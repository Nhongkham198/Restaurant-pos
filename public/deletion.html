User Data Deletion Instructions

To request deletion of your user data, please follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your Facebook account.
  2. Go to "Settings & Privacy" > "Settings" > "Apps and Websites".
  3. Find "POS System" and click "Remove".
  4. This will remove the app's access and signal us to delete your data.

Alternatively, contact us at support@example.com.
